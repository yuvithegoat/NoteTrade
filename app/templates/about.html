About NoteTrade

NoteTrade was founded by two 13-year-olds, Anay Tandon and Yuvraj Wadhan, with one simple goal: to make school notes easier to share, buy, and sell. What started as an idea between friends has now become a fast-growing platform where students can trade knowledge for money.

Our app lets users upload their best revision notes, study guides, and exam resources, and sell them to others who need a boost. Buyers can preview a few pages before purchasing, and sellers earn real money for their hard work — all through a secure, easy-to-use system with wallets, payouts, and more.

We believe that students should be able to help each other succeed, and even earn a little on the side while doing it. Whether you’re prepping for exams or just looking to save time, NoteTrade is here to change the way students learn — one trade at a time.

FAQs

Is NoteTrade free?

Yes! Browsing and previewing is free. We only take a small commission when a note is sold.

Can I sell notes if I’m not in the UK?

Absolutely. We're open to international students too!

How are payments handled?

We use a secure wallet system. You earn credits and withdraw them safely when you’re ready.
